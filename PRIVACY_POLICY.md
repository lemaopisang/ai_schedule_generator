# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI Schedule Generator ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Google account authorization data (when you choose to sign in with Google).
- Calendar access permission (only if you choose to export events to Google Calendar).
- Task data you enter in the app (task name, duration, priority).
- Locally stored app data (saved task list using device local storage).

## 2. How We Use Information

- To generate schedule suggestions from your task input.
- To save your task list locally on your device.
- To export schedule events to your Google Calendar only when you request it.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

- Task data is stored locally on your device using local storage.
- Google authentication tokens are used only for the requested calendar action.
- We do not intentionally store your Google Calendar data on our own servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services, including:

- Google Sign-In
- Google Calendar API
- Gemini API (for schedule generation)

These services are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

## 5. Data Sharing

- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not share your data with third parties except as needed to provide app features you request (for example, creating calendar events through Google APIs).

## 6. Your Choices

- You can choose not to sign in with Google.
- You can choose not to export events to Google Calendar.
- You can remove locally saved tasks by clearing app data.

## 7. Security

We apply reasonable efforts to keep data secure, but no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in this document with a revised "Last updated" date.

## 10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app owner/developer.
